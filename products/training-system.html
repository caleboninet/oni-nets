BUILT FOR HOW
PEOPLE ACTUALLY TRAIN.

Volleyball Clubs

A system that works across club environments—fitting into training spaces where a full-size setup isn't practical.

Coaches

Designed with coaches in mind. Set up focused training drills, ball-control work, and small-sided sessions without the overhead of a full-size system.

Skill Development

Focused environments for athletes working on ball control, technique, and touch. The compact format creates concentrated repetitions.

Individual Athletes

For players who want to train outside of team settings. A system that works in gyms, backyards, and smaller spaces.

COMMON QUESTIONS

When will the Oni Short Court be available?

The first production run is being prepared. Join early access at oninets.com/california to receive availability updates and first-release pricing.

How much smaller is it than a full-size system?

Oni Short Court Training System is approximately one-third the size of a full volleyball court system. Exact dimensions will be shared ahead of the first production release.

Is this suitable for indoor use?

Yes. The Oni Short Court is designed to work in indoor training environments, club facilities, and gym spaces. It was developed with indoor training use in mind.

Can it be used for competitive play?

The Oni Short Court is designed primarily for skill development, ball control, and focused training rather than competitive full-game play. For competitive play, see the full-size Oni System.

What is the price?

Pricing will be confirmed and communicated to early-access members ahead of the first production release.

LOOKING FOR THE FULL-SIZE SYSTEM?

The Oni System is the full-size platform designed for grass and indoor competition with interchangeable base configurations.
